Market Perspective

Strong Buyer Demand for
Specialty Contracting Businesses

30
Years of Sector Focus

Private equity groups and strategic acquirers continue to pursue well-positioned contracting and industrial services companies.

Private equity firms and family offices remain active in fragmented service sectors, pursuing both platform formations and add-on acquisitions.
Strategic acquirers pursue acquisitions to expand capabilities and geographic reach, driving sustained demand for well-positioned contractors in core and adjacent markets.
Infrastructure modernization and grid expansion are driving sustained specialty contractor demand across electrical, mechanical, and utility services sectors.
AI-driven data center construction is increasing demand and valuations for specialty contractors serving electrical infrastructure, mechanical systems, and modular construction.
Well-positioned companies with strong management teams and consistent performance continue to command premium valuations.
